Short Comings In our system

First I want to discuss about what we lacks in India. In short, I just want to highlight in our system we were taught to be a good servant rather than a terrible master. We were taught to go towards safe way rather than to make mistakes and learn from it.
As in every system there are a lot of shortcomings in our system too. More Favorable system towards minorities, 80s education system are very few examples.
We were never taught manners, ethics, and values like Japanese system or being creative, risk taker or enthusiastic like American system in our system, we learn those not in school but from our parents or family.
We are forced to be an encyclopedia and dictionary. I always doubt why a medical student has to give a physics exam why an engineering student needs to give a chemistry exam to take his field in college. Why don’t we check how much he knows in his respective field before joining college? May be for now my knowledge and experience in this field is limited what’s why I am asking such questions but I believe it is somehow true, we lost thousands of Einstein minds because of this. It is terrible for the future of our country and the world.
It is being said that education is the backbone of the future of any country but unfortunately we lacks the most in this area.
Some people may question me, But our great scientist in ISRO (Indian space and Research Organization) and DRDO (Defense Research and Development Organization) Studied the same way as we are studying but they are successful and so intelligent. So we can also be like them. For this argument I just want to say, there are billions of stars in our galaxies but only few are so bright that we could see them from here.
