My ISKCON VISIT

Park

me :)

I visited ISKCON temple Today (25-10-2021) with my friends. It was really a fun experience, met new lovely people and read so many books which were in so many languages like Sanskrit, Hindi, English and Urdu although i bought only one which is Sri Canakya-niti The structure of the temple is soo good and unique,Went inside the temple and took some amazing photos (See above). Spent around 1 hour in the laborary searching and reading ancient books, hoping to buy some good and knowledgible stuffs. One of the things that i liked the most is the bronze sclupture of lord Krishna which was the most amazing sclupure that i ever saw in my entire life. the quality of those things are world class, so beautiful and attrative to watch. Apart from these amazing thins i would also like to mansion that the fragrance inside the book shop and the center part of the temple is uniquely refreshing which indeed refresh your inner soul to peace. There was a lot of visitors and everyone must takout their shoes or sleepers and deposit them in the lockers before entering the main partof the tample to maintain its cleaness and to pay respect to the gods. There was a sweet shop on the other side of the temple with unique sweet to buy and eat.There was also a restaurent on the exit side of the temple where people can eat only vegetarian meals.
There was a auditorium where shows about the Teachings of God Krishna but unfortunately due to the lack of time we couldn't watch it but i would like to watch someday in future. So many beautiful places to hang-out with friends. And the knowledge to obtain peace in life. Like Become as important to others as they deserve. Life too short to waste on unnecessary things like hate, greed and anger. we must learn to love others in everyway possible, be kind to everyone and many more. Inside the temple everything was so cold as ice and refreshing. Like the Prasad of god.
Hence, we stayed there for around 90mins because some of my friends have to go for tuitions but we all agree that this was a very amazing place and we should comeback again sometime in future.
I'm reading this book and would love to share my knowledge.
